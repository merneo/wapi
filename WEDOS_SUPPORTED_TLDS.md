# WEDOS Supported Top-Level Domains (TLD)

This document lists all top-level domains (TLD) supported by WEDOS for domain registration and management.

## Czech and Slovak TLDs

- `.cz` - Czech Republic
- `.sk` - Slovakia

## International Generic TLDs (gTLD)

### Common gTLDs
- `.com` - Commercial
- `.net` - Network
- `.org` - Organization
- `.info` - Information
- `.biz` - Business
- `.name` - Personal names
- `.pro` - Professionals

### Geographic gTLDs
- `.eu` - European Union

## Country Code TLDs (ccTLD)

### European ccTLDs
- `.at` - Austria
- `.be` - Belgium
- `.bg` - Bulgaria
- `.ch` - Switzerland
- `.de` - Germany
- `.dk` - Denmark
- `.es` - Spain
- `.fi` - Finland
- `.fr` - France
- `.gr` - Greece
- `.hu` - Hungary
- `.ie` - Ireland
- `.it` - Italy
- `.li` - Liechtenstein
- `.lu` - Luxembourg
- `.nl` - Netherlands
- `.no` - Norway
- `.pl` - Poland
- `.pt` - Portugal
- `.ro` - Romania
- `.se` - Sweden
- `.uk` - United Kingdom (including `.co.uk`, `.org.uk`, `.me.uk`)

### Other ccTLDs
- `.us` - United States
- `.ca` - Canada
- `.au` - Australia
- `.jp` - Japan
- `.cn` - China
- `.ru` - Russia

## Special TLDs

- `.co` - Colombia (also used as commercial alternative)
- `.io` - British Indian Ocean Territory (popular for tech)
- `.tv` - Tuvalu (popular for media)
- `.me` - Montenegro (popular for personal)
- `.xyz` - Generic
- `.online` - Generic
- `.site` - Generic
- `.store` - Generic
- `.tech` - Generic
- `.cloud` - Generic
- `.app` - Generic (requires HTTPS)
- `.dev` - Generic (requires HTTPS)

## Multi-level TLDs

Some TLDs have second-level domains that are commonly used:

- `.co.uk` - United Kingdom (commercial)
- `.org.uk` - United Kingdom (organizations)
- `.me.uk` - United Kingdom (personal)
- `.com.au` - Australia (commercial)
- `.com.br` - Brazil (commercial)
- `.com.mx` - Mexico (commercial)

## Notes

- This list is based on common TLDs supported by European domain registrars like WEDOS
- Some TLDs may have specific registration requirements (e.g., residency, business registration)
- TLD availability and pricing may vary
- For the most up-to-date list, please refer to WEDOS official documentation or contact WEDOS support
